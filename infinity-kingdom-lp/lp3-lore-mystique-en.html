I. The Broken World

Before the beginning of time as we know it, there existed a kingdom of terrifying beauty — an empire forged by the will of five primordial gods. For millennia, balance reigned. Then came the Fracture.

The lands shattered. The skies fell. And millions of wandering souls were cast into a world without master, without law, without mercy. A world waiting for onlyone thing : someone brave enough to claim it.

II. Your Destiny

You did not arrive here by chance. The Kingdom called to you. It always calls those who carry within them the flame of conquest — those who refuse to bend when the world demands submission.

Parmi fifty thousand warriors, you will find allies, rivals, traitors and perhaps, if the stars smile upon you, a family of blood and iron.
